You cannot watch anything better than this documentary. It documents Bitcoins early days and shows how it continued to rise and fall amidst the Mt. 
Gox or the Cyprus crisis . 
It is filmed and written by Daniel Mross, a computer programmer, and two other Bitcoin enthusiasts Patrick Lope and Nicholas Mross. 
This documentary features some of the very early adopters and startup builders of Bitcoin namely Gavin Andresen, Brian Armstrong, Margaux Avedisian.
 Also, it introduces you to how the mining industry evolved during these years and Bitcoin got its physical face.
